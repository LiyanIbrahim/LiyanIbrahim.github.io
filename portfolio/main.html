I’m currently a third year undergraduate student at NYUAD majoring in Interactive Media and minoring in Computer Science. I graduate in May 2022!

I’m interested in many things (liberal arts got the best of me). However, my main interests primarily encompass digital storytelling, decolonizing arts and technology, and the social implications of the internet.

Over the years, I’ve worked on multiple projects. Those include OpenGulf, a set of interconnected digital projects focusing on historical documentation about the Gulf and I’m currently working with a research team on Decolonizing Interactive Media at NYUAD. I also was a volunteer at this years’ Mozilla Festival.
