حرب/حب (War/Love)

web art



حرب/حب (War/Love) is an interactive web art experience abstracting from both the poetry of Mahmoud Darwish and the Mahmoud Darwish Museum, which is currently in Palestine. The project rethinks physical spaces as resilient vessels for memory and cultural identity, especially vital in times of historic erasure. By abstracting from Darwish's powerful imagery and the museum's space, (War/Love) invites audiences to engage with Palestine's artistic and cultural heritage in a way that transcends geographical boundaries. This digital reimagining serves as a virtual monument, bridging distances while questioning how histories are preserved, erased, or altered, urging viewers to consider the impact of memory, loss, and resilience.
