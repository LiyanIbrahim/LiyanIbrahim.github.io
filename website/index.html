My interests vary and include but are not limited to, digital storytelling, decolonizing arts and technology, and the social implications of the internet. I am currently completing a Google specialization in User Experience Design. I’m very passionate about cool websites and interactive designs!

My primary interests include UX/UI design, digital storytelling, decolonizing arts and technology, and the social implications of the internet. I’m interested in multimedia art and content that has the power to influence someone's emotions, views, and awareness of their surroundings. With a technical background, I am also interested in challenging the notions of how we view and understand new emerging technologies by looking at ways digital tools could be used to elevate the art of storytelling. My capstone project was exhibited at the Mozilla festival as part of their Decolonizing AI space.
