Comic Strip

Storytelling, illustrations, Digital Comics

Birthday Fiesta is an interactive digital comic that blends storytelling with UX/UI design to create an engaging web experience. It reimagines sequential art in a dynamic online format, following a playful, step-by-step baking tutorial with a bright, celebratory theme.

UX/UI Design:
Creating an intuitive, visually cohesive, and interactive storytelling experience.
Visual & Interaction Design:
Developing original 2D illustrations and animations using Photoshop and Illustrator.
Web Interactivity:
Implementing seamless interactions to enhance engagement.
Narrative Strategy:
Structuring a clear, immersive digital story.
