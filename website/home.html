when home won't let you stay

Interactive Experience, Game Design

An Interactive Unity Experience on Memory and Displacement
When Home Won’t Let You Stay is an interactive Unity experience exploring the Palestinian diaspora through immersive storytelling, UX design, and interactive soundscapes. Users navigate a symbolic “box” that mirrors my childhood in the UAE, uncovering objects—an embroidered rug, family photos, and traditional clothing—that reveal a constant connection to Palestine.

Dynamic audio shifts as users explore, featuring ocean waves, a Palestinian lullaby, Ghassan Kanafani’s words, and news reports on journalist Shireen Abu Akleh, creating an emotional, layered narrative. This project reimagines game design as a space for memory, resistance, and liberation.
