How to Make a Human Being?

Digital Storytelling, Video Editing, Stop Motion

This is a video piece that lives on a web page made in a group of 4 students. We adapted a “how to” prompt into giving a tutorial that is unrealistic/unachievable, How to make a human Being?. Since our concept was more abstract that is disguised as something more conventional, the project has both real, physical components as well as animated parts through the videos.
This experience focuses of enhancing the following:
Well-written, clear story - Video + animation/effects production - Video + animation/effects editing - Web implementation
