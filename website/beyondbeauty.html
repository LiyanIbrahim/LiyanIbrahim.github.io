Beyond Beauty

Speculative Design, 3D Animation

This collaborative project presents a compelling short video animation delving into the profound influence of media-driven beauty standards on mental health and mood. Through imaginative storytelling, it envisions a speculative future that vividly illustrates an inclusive world of beauty standards in New York City.
Crafted using cutting-edge techniques, the video employs motion tracking, a blend of mixed media formats, and meticulous video and audio editing utilizing Adobe's After Effects and Premiere Pro. Moreover, it integrates immersive 3D animations developed with Unity, enhancing the visual narrative's depth and impact.
