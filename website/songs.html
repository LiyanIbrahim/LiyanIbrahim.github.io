I want to sing, but I don't know any songs

web art

"I want to sing but I don’t know any songs" is an interactive web art experience, exploring childhood lullabies through UX/UI design, using fragmented visuals and interactive elements to convey themes of war, displacement, and inherited trauma. The interface guides users through a nonlinear journey, allowing them to piece together the silent weight of memories passed down through generations.
The design prioritizes intuitive navigation and emotional storytelling, creating a space where users engage with the duality of lullabies—both comforting and reflective of historical grief. Through motion, visual layering, and interactive storytelling, the experience captures the tension between innocence and loss in a way that is deeply personal and immersive.
